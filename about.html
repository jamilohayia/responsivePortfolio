About Me

My professional background centers in client service and business development. I'm currently a Consultant at ACA Compliance Group, where the goal is to provide risk mitigation and regulatory compliance solutions for companies around the world. I'm also a current student in a Full Stack Coding Bootcamp offered by Columbia University.

I have a deep passion for volunteering, community development, and sustainability. As an Ambassador for the All Ivy Board, I help organize social and professional events for thousands of young professionals in the Greater New York City area. I'm also a member of the Nature Conservancy whose mission is to conserve the lands and waters on which all life depends. I earned my Bachelor of the Arts degree in Political Science from Columbia University in May 2015.
